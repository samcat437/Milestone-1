Weddings

We have a wealth of experience performing wedding ceremonies, cocktail hours and receptions together. We are able to gracefully end the music depending on what's going on in the moment - something that simply pressing play and pause could never achieve! We love that blissful moment when the bride steps into the room and walks down the aisle to her favourite song.

Prior to the ceremony, we will work closely with you via phone and email to find your ideal ceremony music. You can view our wedding ceremony favourites on our repertoire page. Feel free to download this PDF for easy reference.

Prices and Packages

Wedding Ceremony - From £500

We will perform pre-ceremony music 30 minutes before your guests arrive to set the mood, during the ceremony and as you exit the venue as a married couple.

Wedding Ceremony and Cocktail Hour/Reception - From £700

In addition to the ceremony, we will travel to your reception venue and perform a bespoke set list for your guests. We are happy to play indoors and out weather permitting. Just let us know how much time you will need in the enquiry form for a quote.

Wedding Ceremony, Cocktail Hour/Reception, and Wedding Breakfast - From £800

If you would like us to also perform during your wedding breakfast, we are able to further cater our repertoire to add to the ambiance.
